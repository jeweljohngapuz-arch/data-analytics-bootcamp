What to Think When Writing It

Keep it business-focused, not code-focused.

Think in this order:

1️⃣ What decision could a manager make from this?

2️⃣ What pattern did the data show?

3️⃣ What stands out?

Example thinking:

Which region sells the most?
Which product drives revenue?
Which transactions matter most?
--------------------------------------
Example:

Question:
Which transactions are high value?

Answer:
Used conditional logic (if revenue ≥ threshold).

Insight:
Laptop sales consistently appear in the high-value category.
